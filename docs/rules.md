# Rules

## Budget Rules

```text
budget_month = month(consumption_date)
budget_deduction_amount = expense amount
budget_item = valid item from 2026Budget[item]
```

Cash payments deduct budget and cash flow on the consumption date.

Credit-card payments deduct budget on the consumption date and cash flow on the calculated payment date.

Installment payments deduct the full budget amount on the consumption date and split cash flow across payment dates.

## Credit Card Payment Rules

| Credit card | Consumption day | Payment date |
|---|---|---|
| YuShan | 1-12 | 23rd of the same month |
| YuShan | 13 or later | 23rd of the next month |
| Other credit cards | 1-5 | 17th of the same month |
| Other credit cards | 6 or later | 17th of the next month |

## Installment Rules

Installments are generated by average split.

If the amount cannot be evenly divided:

```text
installments 1 to N-1 = floor(total amount / installment count)
installment N = total amount - sum(previous installments)
```

Payment schedule rows may be manually corrected during reconciliation.

## Learning Rules

Payment method and budget item rules use accumulated confirmation history.

A single manual change does not update the default rule.

Default suggestions are updated only when recent history supports the change, such as 4 of the latest 5 confirmations matching the same value.

## Classification Status

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| auto_confirmed | Rule confidence is high enough to use directly |
| needs_review | The system has a suggestion, but the user should confirm |
| manually_confirmed | The user confirmed or changed the value |
| unable_to_classify | No reasonable suggestion exists |

## Budget Alert Rules

| Usage ratio | Status |
|---:|---|
| Less than 70% | normal |
| 70% to below 90% | reminder |
| 90% to below 100% | warning |
| 100% or above | over_budget |

Over-budget items must be highlighted most prominently.
